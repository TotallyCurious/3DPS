Our Services

Specialized in architecture, we provide 3D printed architectural miniature models and prototypes as well as non-architectural models based on our customers’ requirements.

Our products are made using lightweight durable thermoplastic materials such as ABS, PLA, HIPS, etc. which are recyclable, thus being environment friendly.

Products that are to be discarded can be sent back to us for recycling, free of charge. Therefore, they can be reused and further eliminates any hassles on waste management.
